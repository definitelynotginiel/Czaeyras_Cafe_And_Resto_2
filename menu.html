About our Menu

Our Menu list was created by Mrs. Czarina Atienza, the founder of Czaeyra's Cafe and Resto. She is an experience Cafe worker and Barista and also knowledgeable in making various types of beverages and food recipes. Since the opening of Czaeyra's Cafe and Resto, Mrs. Czarina was accompanied by Ms. Heidi, also an experienced cook.

Customers can choose from a variety of flavors for Chicken Wings: Plain, Soy Garlic, Honey Siracha, Salted Egg, Spicy Salted Egg, Mango Habanero, Cheesy Jalapeño and Buffalo. For the Fries, customers can choose from: Cheese, Sour and Cream and Barbecue.
